[Story]Enter the Village Chief's House to find Doctor Han, and also find Ling'er. It turns out Ling'er had collapsed from pregnancy and was rescued by Doctor Han, but Ling'er told Doctor Han not to let Li Xiaoyao know. So Doctor Han gives Li Xiaoyao a prescription to treat Ling'er. At this point, Yueru tells Xiaoyao that she can get Ginseng, Snow Lotus, and Fleeceflower Root, but Xiaoyao needs to figure out the rest on his own. So the two split up to search for the herbs. Here's how to get them:
 (1) Ginkgo Nut: On the tree behind the Village Chief's House.
 (2) Carp: Borrow a Fishing Rod from the Fisherman's House in Baihe Village, then fish at the bridge to the lower-left. After catching the Carp, remember to return the Fishing Rod to the fisherman. You can keep it, but it will take up an item slot and make item selection more troublesome.
 (3) Deer Antler: Go to Baihe Village South Outskirts, pick up the Beast Trap on the road. Then find the wild deer and observe its escape route. Place the Beast Trap on that route and chase the deer. When the deer is caught by the Beast Trap, take the Deer Antler and release the deer.

Doctor Han adds that he sent people to request Master Zhixiu to come down the mountain, but everyone who met the abbot has become a monk there, even his future son-in-law Jiang Shaoyun.
